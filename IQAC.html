Internal Quality Assurance Cell (IQAC)

The IQAC at SSS College for Women works to channelize efforts towards quality enhancement in academics and administration. It coordinates quality-related activities, including continuous improvement in teaching-learning processes, student support services, and institutional planning.

📊

Quality Audits

Regular internal audits of academic and administrative processes.

📋

Feedback Systems

Structured feedback from students and faculty for continuous improvement.

🎯

Strategic Planning

Long-term planning for institutional growth and academic excellence.
