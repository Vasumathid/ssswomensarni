About the College

Where Every Woman Is Encouraged to Rise

SSS College for Women opened its doors in 2022-23 at Aagaram Village, Arni Taluk, under the AKN Educational Trust founded by Chairman Thiru A.K. Natarajan — who also leads the well-established SSS College of Arts, Science and Management, Arcot (since 2005).

This is a campus built exclusively for women — a safe, confidence-building space where students are pushed to grow intellectually, professionally, and personally. Under Dr. V. VijayaLakshmi, M.Com., M.Phil.,MBA., Ph.D., our Principal, an experienced faculty team blends modern infrastructure with strong, grounded values.

Beyond the classroom, our students take part in industrial visits, rural camps, entrepreneurial ventures, cultural events, and sports — stepping out as confident, well-rounded, socially responsible women ready for what's next.
