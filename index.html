About the College

A Women's College Built for Excellence

SSS College for Women was established in 2022-23 at Aagaram Village, Arni Taluk, under the AKN Educational Trust founded by Chairman Thiru A.K. Natarajan — who also runs the well-established SSS College of Arts, Science and Management, Arcot (since 2005).

The college is dedicated exclusively to women, providing a safe, empowering environment where students grow intellectually, professionally, and personally. Led by Dr. S. Lakshmi, M.Com., M.Phil., Ph.D. as Principal, the college combines experienced faculty, modern infrastructure, and strong values.

Our students participate in industrial visits, rural camps, entrepreneurial ventures, cultural activities, and sports — developing into well-rounded, socially responsible citizens.
