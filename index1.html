About SSS College

Founded to Empower Women

SSS College for Women, Arni was established in 2022-23 at Aagaram Village, Arni Taluk, under the AKN Educational Trust founded by Chairman Thiru A.K. Natarajan — who also runs the well-established SSS College of Arts, Science and Management, Arcot (since 2005).

Led by Dr. S. Lakshmi, M.Com., M.Phil., Ph.D. as Principal, the college combines experienced faculty, modern infrastructure, and strong values in an exclusively women's environment.

Students participate in industrial visits, rural camps, entrepreneurial ventures, cultural activities, live projects, news analysis and sports — developing into well-rounded, socially responsible citizens.

Mr. A.K. Natarajan
Chairman — SSS Group of Institutions
Since 2005, Thiru A.K. Natarajan established AKN Educational Trust at Arcot and began SSS College of Arts, Science and Management — starting with 150 students, now hosting 1,850 students with nearly 80 staff members.

In 2018, the Trust started SSS Matriculation School which expanded to Plus Two by 2021. In 2022–23, the Trust began SSS College for Women in Agaram Village, Arni Taluk, Thiruvannamalai District.
