Leadership

Chairman's Message

Mr. A.K. Natarajan Chairman, SSS Group of Institutions
AKN Educational Trust, Arcot

Thiru A.K. Natarajan is the Chairman of AKN Educational Trust. A visionary leader, he established the Educational Trust in 2005 at Arcot with his family members as Trustees, starting the SSS College of Arts, Science and Management, Arcot with 150 students — which today has grown to over 1,850 students and nearly 80 staff (Teaching & Non-Teaching).

In 2018, he expanded the trust further by establishing SSS Matriculation School at Arcot — starting from LKG to VIII standard with 100 students, later extended to Class 10 and then +2 in 2021, now with 300 students.

In the course of 2022-23, driven by his commitment to women's empowerment and rural education, Thiru Natarajan started SSS College for Women at Aagaram Village, Arni Taluk, Thiruvannamalai District — the newest jewel in the SSS Group of Institutions.

His mission is simple: to bring quality higher education to women in rural Tamil Nadu, enabling them to become self-reliant, socially responsible citizens and leaders.

"Education is the most powerful tool we can give to our daughters. SSS College for Women exists to ensure no woman in this district is left behind."

— Thiru A.K. Natarajan, Chairman

Principal's Message

👤 Dr. S. Lakshmi Replace with actual photo
Dr. S. Lakshmi M.Com., M.Phil., Ph.D.
Principal, SSS College for Women, Arni

SSS College for Women was founded by Chairman Thiru A.K. Natarajan with a clear mission — to provide education to women from different strata of society, imparting comprehensive education with in-depth knowledge not only in their respective disciplines but also in related areas.

This institution, with its diverse and dynamic community of students, offers a unique combination of excellent graduates and accomplished faculty. SSS College for Women provides a supportive and empowering environment. This college creates a strong sense of community among female students and provides many opportunities for leadership development, attitudes, and practical experience — all of which are necessary to take up social responsibilities in society.

Student life includes participation in sports, cultural activities, and social service. Faculty members help students shape their future. SSS College for Women is an aesthetically designed building with state-of-the-art computer lab, internet access, auditorium, a well-stocked digital library, and sports facilities.

With so much to offer, it is natural that students of SSS College for Women get a unique opportunity to shine themselves in their respective field of study. This enables them to become well-rounded citizens, fully qualified to choose a profession in society.

"Our goal is not just to educate — it is to empower. Every student who walks through our gates carries the potential to transform her family, her community, and our nation."

— Dr. S. Lakshmi, Principal

Secretary / Manager's Profile

Mr. A.N. Sankar M.E., M.B.A., M.Sc.(IT), LL.B.
Secretary / Manager

Mr. A.N. Sankar, son of Chairman Thiru A.K. Natarajan, serves as the Secretary and Manager of SSS College for Women, Arni. With qualifications spanning Engineering, Business Administration, Information Technology, and Law, he brings a multidisciplinary perspective to college management.

He plays a key role in the day-to-day administration, academic planning, infrastructure development and student welfare at the college.
