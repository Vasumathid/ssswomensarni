Admissions 2025–26

How to Apply

Simple 4-step process. Admissions are open now — limited seats available. Apply early to secure your place.

1

Check Eligibility

Passed HSC (Class 12) from any recognised board. Check course-specific subject requirements in the fee table above.

2

Contact the College

Call 98942 18555 or visit the college office at Aagaram Village, Arni. Collect the application form.

3

Submit Documents

HSC Marksheet, TC, Community Certificate, Aadhaar Card, Passport-size Photos, and Income Certificate if applicable.

4

Pay & Confirm

Pay the semester fee at the college office and complete enrollment. Collect your ID card and fee receipt.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Is SSS College for Women exclusively for women students?
Yes. SSS College for Women is exclusively for women students. This focused environment is designed to give women the confidence, support, and resources they need to excel academically and professionally.
Which university is the college affiliated to?
SSS College for Women, Arni is affiliated to Thiruvalluvar University, the state university for Vellore and Thiruvannamalai districts of Tamil Nadu.
Is there a fee concession for students who scored high in HSC?
Yes. Students who scored 1000 marks and above in HSC get a 15% concession in tuition fee every semester. Students who scored 900 and above get a 10% concession. This applies to every semester throughout the course.
What government scholarships are available?
Students are eligible for BC/MBC/SC/ST scholarships from the Tamil Nadu government, first-generation graduate scholarships, and other state and central government scholarship schemes based on eligibility.
What is the last date to apply for 2025-26?
Admissions are currently open. Seats are limited — we recommend applying as early as possible. Call 98942 18555 or 84380 01755 to check current seat availability and deadlines.
What documents are needed for admission?
You will need: HSC Marksheet (original + copies), Transfer Certificate (TC), Community Certificate, Aadhaar Card, Income Certificate (if applying for scholarship), and 4 passport-size photographs.
Is hostel accommodation available?
Yes. Hostel is available with separate cots, mobile facility, WiFi, entertainment, dispensary, and private safety & security. Contact the college at 98942 18555 for hostel seat availability and fees.
