Computer Lab

Entirely Equipped AC Computer Lab

Our computer laboratory is a state-of-the-art, air-conditioned facility equipped with advanced computers and the latest software. It provides 24-hour internet access to all students.

Solar backup is facilitated to ensure uninterrupted power supply — so students never have to stop their work due to power cuts.

  • Fully air-conditioned for comfortable long-study sessions
  • High-speed internet available 24 hours
  • Solar power backup — uninterrupted access
  • Latest computers with updated software
  • Separate lab for Computer Science and BCA students
  • Faculty supervision and technical support

Library

Well-Stocked & Fully Automated

The SSS College library is completely automated with an enormous collection of books, magazines, and journals across all disciplines. Students can easily search, borrow, and return books through the digital system.

The library serves as a quiet, focused learning space where students can study, research, and prepare for examinations with access to reference materials beyond their textbooks.

  • Thousands of books across all departments
  • Current magazines and academic journals
  • Fully automated issue & return system
  • Digital catalogue for easy book search
  • Reading room with quiet study space
  • Reference section for exam preparation

Chemistry Laboratory

Fully Equipped for Advanced Science

The Chemistry laboratory at SSS College is equipped with all instruments needed for practical experiments in Organic, Inorganic, and Physical Chemistry. Students get comprehensive hands-on training that prepares them for examinations and real-world applications.

  • Full set of glassware and chemical reagents
  • Safety equipment and ventilation
  • Separate benches for individual experiments
  • Modern instruments and analytical tools
  • Faculty-supervised practical sessions
  • Dedicated space for B.Sc. Chemistry students
